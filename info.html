Safety Guidelines

  • Always stand behind the yellow line while waiting for trains
  • Keep your belongings close and be aware of your surroundings
  • Follow all posted signs and instructions from metro staff
  • In case of emergency, use the emergency intercoms on platforms and trains
  • Do not attempt to board or alight when doors are closing

Smart Card Information

Benefits of Smart Card

  • 10% discount on all journeys
  • Auto top-up facility available
  • Valid for 1 year from last recharge
  • Can be used for parking payment

How to Get a Smart Card

  1. Visit any metro station customer care
  2. Fill out the application form
  3. Pay ₹100 (₹50 refundable deposit)
  4. Minimum recharge: ₹100

Metro Rules & Etiquette

  • No eating or drinking inside trains
  • Give up seats for elderly, pregnant women, and differently-abled
  • Keep the metro clean
  • Do not play loud music
  • Photography/filming requires prior permission
